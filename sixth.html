Crochet hook

First you gonna do is probably asking yourself, which size, material, shape and lenght your first crochet hook should be. It won`t be surprising, that there is no right answer to this question. It all depends mostly on which meterial you want work with. There are many types of yarn width, aswell as material type that you can choose from. Thats where you shold focus on first.
